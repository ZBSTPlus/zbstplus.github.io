ZettaByte Plus: Ed Tech Startup

What We Do

ZettaByte Plus is an Ed tech startup that offers free trainings and internships on various tools and technologies, including:

  • Python
  • Golang
  • JavaScript
  • Introduction to Cloud Computing and Cloud Services offered by Various cloud providers ( AWS and Azure)
  • Introduction to Microservices: REST, gRPC and GraphQL
  • Cryptography and Network Security

Our Impact

ZettaByte Plus has conducted over 200 workshops and trained over 100 interns. Our students have gone on to land jobs at top tech companies like Google, Microsoft, and Amazon.
